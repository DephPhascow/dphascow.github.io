Privacy Policy – Ortho Messenger

Last updated: 02 June 2026

Ortho Messenger (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a messaging service that allows users to communicate with each other. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information: username, phone number, profile details.
  • Messages and media: content you send and receive through the service.
  • Device information: device model, operating system, app version.
  • Usage data: app interactions, logs, and basic analytics.

We do not collect more data than is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain Ortho Messenger.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Detect, prevent, and address security or technical issues.
  • Provide user support and respond to requests.

We do not sell your personal data or use it for third‑party advertising.

3. Message Privacy

Your messages are processed only for the purpose of delivering them to the intended recipients. We do not use your private conversations for profiling or marketing.

4. Data Sharing

We may share information only in the following cases:

  • When required by applicable law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the service.
  • With trusted service providers (such as hosting or infrastructure) who process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system can be completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data where appropriate.
  • Restrict or object to certain types of processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Ortho Messenger is not intended for children under the age where they cannot legally consent to data processing in their jurisdiction. If we learn that we have collected data from such a child, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates the latest revision. Continued use of Ortho Messenger after changes means you accept the updated policy.
